About

I am a Health copywriter. I help health companies and E-Commerce business owners write conversion copy to sell their health supplements.

I help health companies to write powerful engaging content on their blog posts.

I understand the cold, warm and hot audience tactics and I educate and compel them to buy the supplement whenever I write a copy for them.

I research the level of market awareness and competitive market sophistication of a health product before writing the copy to get the best of the prospect's trust and beliefs.

I also write email campaigns for the health brand because email marketing for health products works very well.

One thing I will always do is if I work with you, I will give your product a unique mechanism in my copy to differentiate yours from others and make more sales.

I also do a lot of testing in terms of ads and email campaigns to get the best results for the marketing campaign.
